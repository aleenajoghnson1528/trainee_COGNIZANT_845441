From Paradise Biryani | Perungudi 'Paradise started its journey in September 1953 from telangana as a small 100 seater restaurant mainly to serve cinema-goers of the Paradise Theatre. A six-decade-old legacy, the restaurant brand has today gained worldwide acceptance from celebrities and the common man alike, thanks to its single-minded focus on quality with consistency, hygiene and customer service. The Tagline World’s Favourite Biryani is apt in terms of brand recognition and preference amongst the millions of Biryani lovers in India and abroad. Paradise brand is synonymous with Hyderabad and today serves varieties of cuisines like Biryani, Kebabs, Haleem, Indian & Chinese Dishes, Desserts to name a few. Today, Paradise is spread across 7 cities in India.'
